################
Katzenweihnacht
################

*English translation "A Cat's Christmas".*

:Subtitle: Eine Geschichte
:Written by: Edith Schreiber-Wicke
:Illustrated by: Monika Laimgruber
:Published: 1990
:ISBN: 3-219-10482-7

A Christmas story about Novalis, the cat.


Notes
=====

The Christ Child (*Christkind*) is grammatically neuter in German (*es*), and in
Austrian/German tradition is usually pictured as an angelic child rather than the
infant Jesus. The English translation refers to the Christ Child as "he",
following the English sense of the name; it could be rendered neutral instead.
